## Appointments and queue

- Each appointment slot is **15 minutes** long.
- The first appointment of the day starts at **9:00 AM**; the next patient gets 9:15 AM, then 9:30 AM, and so on.
- Each doctor has a **separate schedule** — one doctor being busy does not block another doctor.
- When you ask about your **queue** or **appointment time**, share your **mobile number** (the same number used when booking). We can look up your booked date and time.
- If you booked without a phone number, use the **guest code** we gave you to look up your appointment.

## Booking flow

After describing your symptoms, the assistant will ask for your preferred day and contact phone, tell you which type of visit applies (general, pediatrics, or cardiology), and offer to book an appointment automatically when you confirm.
